Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to commonly asked questions about membership

How often do teams train?

Senior teams typically train twice per week, on Tuesday and Thursday evenings from 7-9pm. Youth teams train once per week, with the day varying depending on the age group. Additional pre-match training sessions may be scheduled during the season.

Do you have payment plans available?

Yes, we offer monthly payment plans for all membership types. This can be arranged with our membership secretary once your application is approved. We also offer discounts for low-income families and students.

I'm new to rugby. Can I still join?

Absolutely! We welcome players of all experience levels. Our coaches provide comprehensive training suitable for beginners, and we have specific introductory sessions throughout the year for new players.

What equipment do I need to bring?

For training sessions, you'll need appropriate footwear (rugby boots with studs for outdoor training), comfortable sports clothing, a gum shield, and a water bottle. Match kit is provided as part of your membership.

Is there an age limit for joining?

We have teams for all ages starting from under 6s. Our mini rugby program caters for ages 6-12, youth teams for 12-18, and senior teams for adults. We also have a thriving veterans team for players over 35.

What's included in the membership fee?

Membership includes match fees, training sessions, club kit, insurance, access to facilities, physiotherapy services (for injuries sustained during club activities), and social membership benefits including discounts at the club bar.
